Extracurriculars

Participation

Which of these activities/groups/clubs were you part of?



Almost 35% respondents claimed to have been associated with technical clubs or activities. Sports and cultural enthusiasts were 29.2% and 26.2% respectively. Amongst the independent bodies Mood Indigo had maximum participation of 27.7% followed by Techfest at 23.8%.

Skills Developed

Did IIT help you develop/pick-up any skill? If yes, then what?


Surprisingly organisational skills grabbed the top spot at 64%. The institute helped 45.2% pick-up technical skills while sports and cultural skills were developed by 36.1% and 34.9% respectively.

Value Addition


Lifelong value addition was the popular choice opted for by 230 (69.3%) graduating students. 12.3% claimed they learnt a lot but were unsure of its relevance in the future while 14.5% did not respond. Only 1 respondent claimed he/she did not perceive anything at all

Influence of POR

Taking up a position of responsibility has greatly benefited your personality and/or helped you secure a lucrative job offer/admission.


When it comes to extracurriculars, IIT Bombay is one of the best platforms to showcase your talent. Events ranging from Mood Indigo and Techfest dubbed as Asia’s largest cultural and technology festivals to PAF and fierce tech/sports GCs, insti has something on it’s plate for everyone. Due to encouragement from seniors and professors alike participation of students in extracurriculars is at all time high and this survey depicts just that.
