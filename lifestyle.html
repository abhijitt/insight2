Lifestyle

Alcohol

How frequently did you drink? (on an average)

33.1% consumed alcohol once or twice during the semester. 30% abstained from alcohol at all costs. A visit to a bar was a once in a month affair for 19.1% respondents while 13.6% refrained from consuming alcohol more than once a week.

What was your favourite place to drink?

Of the 228 respondents that did consume alcohol atleast once, Sunny, a local bar outside IIT Bombay, was the favourite place for 33.3%. Laxmi, a pure veg bar (that’s a rare species!) followed closely at 29.8%. 14% found solace while consuming alcohol in their rooms itself.

Physical and Mental Fitness

Have you ever had to consult a psychiatrist/counselor for mental illness?

91.5% never consulted a psychiatrist for any sort of mental illness. However, 7%, although a small number, did require mental counselling for a brief period.

What is your attitude towards physical fitness?

11.4% considered themselves to be fitness freaks. A major fraction (64.2%) were much more liberal and found it good enough if someone was participating a physical activity once or twice a week. IDGAF was the attitude of 18.7% of the respondents.

Nicotine

How frequently do/did you smoke cigarettes?


“I don’t smoke” said 72.3% seniors proudly. A majority (51.1%) of the people who smoked, smoked hardly once in two or three weeks.
Of those 92 seniors who did smoke, almost 73% started smoking after their freshmen year.

Which of these peer pressures have you faced while at IIT?


OK, hold your breath! The top spot was grabbed by ‘PEER PRESSURE TO STUDY/LEARN’ which was what 35.8% of our respondents faced. Almost 30% experienced peer pressure to participate in extracurriculars or to consume alcohol.

Expenditure

Where do you peg your monthly spending?

79.5% claimed that food was their major cause of spending. 9.5% attributed their spending to alcohol and other intoxicants. The so-called ‘ghati’ junta of the institute spent most of their money in travelling.

Bucket-list

Which of these have you ticked off your bucket-list ?


52.4% experienced the classic dream of every college student: a road trip to Goa with friends.However, 70% did travel ticketless on a local train. 55.7%, after being inspired by Casino Royale, played poker/blackjack.

Which of these places have you visited during your stay at IIT-Bombay?


73% students had been to the VMCC terrace as well as the overhyped boathouse. 62% completed the walk-in-the-park trek to Sameer Hill. Surprisingly, 49% have never been inside H10 while 16.2% have never visited the central library of the institute.
